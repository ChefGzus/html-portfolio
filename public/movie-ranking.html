My top 3 movies of all-time

This whole series is insane!! The music, production and acting are top notch. The story line, along with the animation will take you down a road that has never been traveled down before.

This martial artist is legandary!! He was way ahead of his time regarding physical fitness, and knowlege of mixed martial arts. His skills make him seem god-like. Not to mention his philosophies.
